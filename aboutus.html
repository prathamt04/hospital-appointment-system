advanced technology

microscope

Improved visualization and better illumination. Increases the success of all treatments. Increased precision.

US FDA appr. laser hair reduction

Improved visualization and better illumination. Increases the success of all treatments. Increased precision.

Switch laser

Removes skin darkness caused either by age or sun exposure. Ensures quick service with absolutely no downtime. Pigmentation removal by carbon peeling. Best in use for tattoo removal.

dental diode

Most competent, painless, fastest surgical tool. Ensures faster healing treatment. Used to disinfect root canals, gum tissues etc. Useful in chronic pain therapy, Oral ulcer treatments. Used for advanced biopsies.

digital x-rays

Reduced radiation of the patient compared with classic X-Ray upto 80%. Immediate imaging on the computer screen. High quality digital image that can be analyzed and processed. Images can be easily transferred to the patients. Children friendly due to reduced radiation.

dental implants & physiodispenser

Ensures better precision and placement of the dental implants. Prevents spreading of infection into the operation wound. Increases success of the dental implants.

apex locator

Determines the position of the apical constriction. Determines the length of the root canal space.

endometer

Newest technique of root canal shaping. Increased precision and success of root canal. Automatic reverse functions reduce file breakage.

endo activator

Effective ultrasonic root canal cleaning methods. Plays crucial role in debridement and disinfection of the root canal system. Removal of residual obturation materials during retreatment procedure.

obtruation system

Establishes a seal between the root canal space and the surrounding tissue. Establishes a 3-D filling of the root canal so that there is no bacterial contamination. Performs root canal treatment efficiently. Reduces patient’s anxiety and gives smile on their faces.

full mouth rehabilitation equipment

Advanced treatment to plan & record interrelationship between teeth, muscles and jaws for precise full mouth rehabilitation. Reconstructs natural teeth of the upper jaw & the lower jaw.

PRP (Centrifuge)

Separates the plasma from the platelets and other components. Best for skin rejuvenation and hair regrowth.

oxygen therapy machine

Delivers fresh oxygen to the skin cells. Rejuvenates skin deeply. Ensures skin lightening and brightening.

mesotherapy machine

Helps form meso cocktails to revitalize delicate skin. Removes pigmentation and stretch mark. Prevents stretch hair fall, hair thinning and patchy hair.

radiofrequency skin tightening

Uses RF energy to heat tissue and stimulate subdermal collagen production. Reduces the appearance of fine lines and loose skin.

e-facial

This non-invasive equipment helps to rejuvenate skin deeply. Helps skin lift and removes skin pigmentation. Ensures quick and good treatment .

hydra facial

Hydra facial uses water sprays to regenerate skin, removes dead skin. Increases hydration level of the skin making it soft and supple. Eliminates a variety of blemishes and removes redness of skin.

MDA machine

Super-fine crystals and diamond crystals to exfoliate skin. Removes the top damaged layers of skin to reveal fresh, smooth, glowing skin underneath.

derma rollers

Creates tiny punctures into the skin to stimulate skin repair and cell regeneration. Naturally increases the levels of collagen in the skin to reduce the signs of ageing. Repairs scars and stretch marks.

derma pen

Advanced skin needling machine. Uses multiple needles that vertically pierce the skin. Boosts collagen production. Treats wrinkles, scars, uneven skin tone and stretch marks.

advanced electrosurgical equipment

Ideal for surgeries that require precision. Combines excellent performance of monopolar and bipolar modes. Helps in removal of warts and skin tags.
